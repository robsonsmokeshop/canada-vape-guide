## Canadian Vaping Regulations

All vape products sold in Canada must comply with the Tobacco and Vaping Products Act:
- Maximum nicotine concentration: 20mg/mL
- Maximum pod/tank capacity: 2mL for pre-filled devices (provincial)
- Child-resistant packaging required
- Mandatory health warnings on all packaging
- Available to adults 19+ in British Columbia
